Common questions

Everything you want to know before you call.

Does it really handle painter inquiries? +

Yes. The AI is trained on painting-specific terminology (cut-lines, two-coat prep, sheen, primer, oil vs. latex, caulking and patching, exterior vs. interior square footage). It asks the right qualification questions (rooms, stories, timeline, color guidance needed) so you only drive out to serious in-home estimates.

Why is it $79/month when Ruby charges $235+? +

Ruby uses human operators. Humans cost money per minute. VoiceClaw uses AI, which doesn't. You get a flat rate instead of variable per-call billing. If you're in a paint-store co-op program you already use (Sherwin-Williams Preferred Contractor, Benjamin Moore Pro), it can be even less.

Does it lock me to one paint brand? +

No. VoiceClaw is brand-neutral. You buy from Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, PPG, Dunn-Edwards, Kelly-Moore, or whichever paint store you prefer. We don't change what you specify. If you later tap a paint-store preferred-contractor benefit, great. But it's not a requirement.

What happens when someone calls my old number? +

You can either keep your existing number and forward overflow or after-hours calls to VoiceClaw, or port your number fully. Most painters start with forwarding to test through exterior season, then port once they trust it. We handle the telecom piece; you don't touch a carrier.

Can it actually book into my existing schedule? +

Yes. VoiceClaw syncs with PaintScout, Jobber, Housecall Pro, EstimateRocket, Google Calendar, and most field-service tools painters use. If you run on paper or SMS, it can text you the booking details and confirm with the homeowner directly until you catch up.

How is the setup actually under 5 minutes? Other tools take weeks. +

Other tools make you configure a dashboard. VoiceClaw asks you about your business in a phone conversation and configures itself from what you say. No forms, no logins, no dashboard to learn. If you can describe your painting business to a new homeowner at a walkthrough, you can set VoiceClaw up.

Does the $79 hold through the slow shoulder season? +

$79 is the standard flat rate year-round. But we know painter cash flow swings hard, plentiful in July, tight in February. If you want to pause through your two slowest months each year, we can do that. Your phone number and website stay live, the AI receptionist comes back when you do. Just ask on your first call.

Can I cancel? +

Yes. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. Your phone number ports back out. We keep your logs and transcripts accessible for 90 days after cancellation.
